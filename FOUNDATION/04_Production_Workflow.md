\# 04\_Production\_Workflow.md



\# AI Documentary Channel — Production Workflow



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the complete production pipeline for creating every documentary episode.



The workflow is designed to keep production:



\* Organized

\* Scalable

\* Consistent

\* High quality



Every episode follows the same process.



\---



\# Production Pipeline Overview



```

Idea Selection



↓



Research



↓



Story Development



↓



Script Writing



↓



Character Design



↓



Asset Creation



↓



Storyboard / Blueprint



↓



AI Generation



↓



Editing



↓



Quality Control



↓



Publishing

```



\---



\# PHASE 01 — Topic Selection



\## Goal



Choose a story with strong audience potential.



\---



\## Requirements



The topic must have:



\* Strong narrative

\* Reliable information

\* Visual possibilities

\* Emotional impact



\---



\## Output



Created:



Topic Proposal



Including:



\* Title idea

\* Category

\* Main question

\* Why this story matters



\---



\# PHASE 02 — Research



\## Goal



Collect accurate information.



\---



\## Research Sources



Use:



\* Official documents

\* Historical archives

\* Books

\* Academic sources

\* Reliable journalism



\---



\## Research Categories



Collect:



\### Timeline



Important events in chronological order.



\---



\### People



Important individuals.



\---



\### Locations



Important places.



\---



\### Evidence



Confirmed information.



\---



\### Theories



Different explanations.



\---



\## Output



File:



01\_Research.md



\---



\# PHASE 03 — Script Development



\## Goal



Transform research into a cinematic narrative.



\---



\## Script Structure



\### Hook



First 30-60 seconds.



Purpose:



Create curiosity.



\---



\### Introduction



Explain:



\* Time

\* Place

\* Situation



\---



\### Development



Build:



\* Conflict

\* Investigation

\* Discovery



\---



\### Conclusion



Create:



\* Emotional ending

\* Reflection

\* Remaining questions



\---



\## Output



File:



02\_Script.md



\---



\# PHASE 04 — Character Design



\## Goal



Create consistent digital characters.



\---



\## Process



For every important character define:



\* Identity

\* Appearance

\* Personality

\* Clothing

\* Voice

\* Behavior



\---



\## Output



File:



03\_Characters.md



\---



\# PHASE 05 — Asset Creation



\## Goal



Prepare all non-character production elements.



\---



\## Includes



\* Locations

\* Objects

\* Vehicles

\* Documents

\* Audio references

\* Visual style rules



\---



\## Output



File:



04\_Assets.md



\---



\# PHASE 06 — Blueprint Creation



\## Goal



Convert the story into production instructions.



\---



\## Blueprint Contains



\* Scenes

\* Shots

\* Camera direction

\* Lighting

\* Audio

\* Editing notes



\---



\## Rules



Blueprint never repeats:



\* Character descriptions

\* Asset descriptions

\* Prompts



It only references IDs.



\---



\## Output



File:



05\_Blueprint.md



\---



\# PHASE 07 — AI Production



\## Goal



Generate visual and audio materials.



\---



\## Production Order



Recommended:



1\. Generate reference images

2\. Lock character consistency

3\. Generate environments

4\. Generate video shots

5\. Generate narration

6\. Create sound design



\---



\# PHASE 08 — Editing



\## Goal



Combine all elements into a cinematic documentary.



\---



\## Editing Priorities



\### Story First



Never sacrifice storytelling for visuals.



\---



\### Pacing



Maintain:



\* Curiosity

\* Emotional progression

\* Viewer retention



\---



\### Sound



Audio quality has equal importance to visuals.



\---



\# PHASE 09 — Quality Control



Before publishing check:



\---



\## Story



\* Is information accurate?

\* Is the timeline correct?



\---



\## Visuals



\* Are characters consistent?

\* Are historical details correct?



\---



\## Audio



\* Is narration clear?

\* Are sound effects realistic?



\---



\## Viewer Experience



\* Does the opening hook work?

\* Is the pacing engaging?

\* Is the ending memorable?



\---



\# PHASE 10 — Publishing



\## Upload Checklist



Before publishing:



\### Title



Clear + curiosity driven.



\---



\### Thumbnail



Readable and emotional.



\---



\### Description



Contains:



\* Summary

\* Keywords

\* Sources



\---



\### Metadata



Complete:



\* Tags

\* Category

\* Chapters



\---



\# Post-Publishing Analysis



Review:



\* Click-through rate

\* Average view duration

\* Audience retention

\* Comments



Use data to improve future episodes.



\---



\# Continuous Improvement



Every episode should improve at least one area:



\* Storytelling

\* Visual quality

\* Production speed

\* Audience engagement



\---



\# End of Production Workflow
