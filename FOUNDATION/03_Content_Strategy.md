\# AI Documentary Channel ‚Äî Content Strategy



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the content selection strategy, episode categories, audience growth direction, and long-term publishing plan.



The goal is to create a scalable documentary channel with consistent quality and a recognizable identity.



\---



\# Content Philosophy



Every episode must satisfy three requirements:



\## 1. Strong Story



The event must contain:



\* Conflict

\* Mystery

\* Human decisions

\* Unexpected outcomes



\---



\## 2. Visual Potential



The story must allow cinematic reconstruction through:



\* 3D environments

\* Historical scenes

\* Maps

\* Animations

\* Evidence visualization



\---



\## 3. Audience Interest



The topic should naturally create curiosity.



The viewer should ask:



"What really happened?"

\# Topic Selection Framework



Before approving an episode, evaluate:



\---



\## Story Score



Question:



Does the story have a powerful narrative?



Score:



1-10



\---



\## Mystery Score



Question:



Does the audience want answers?



Score:



1-10



\---



\## Visual Score



Question:



Can this become a cinematic documentary?



Score:



1-10



\---



\## Emotional Score



Question:



Will viewers remember it?



Score:



1-10



\---



\# Episode Approval Rule



A topic should usually score:

8/10 or higher
