# AI Documentary Channel — Project Vision

---

# Project Overview

This project is a cinematic AI-powered documentary channel focused on creating high-quality storytelling experiences using:

* Artificial Intelligence
* 3D scene generation
* Cinematic narration
* Historical research
* Advanced visual storytelling

The goal is to transform real-world mysteries, historical events, crimes, disasters and unexplained phenomena into immersive documentary episodes.

---

# Core Identity

## Channel Type

Cinematic Documentary Channel

## Main Categories

* True Crime
* Historical Mysteries
* Unsolved Cases
* Disasters
* Aviation Mysteries
* Hidden Events
* Historical Investigations

---

# Creative Philosophy

The channel does not create simple AI videos.

The goal is to create:

> "A cinematic documentary experience where storytelling, visuals and sound work together like a professional production studio."

Every episode should feel:

* Authentic
* Emotional
* Immersive
* Historically grounded
* Cinematically directed

---

# Audience Experience

The viewer should feel:

* Curiosity
* Suspense
* Discovery
* Emotional connection

The audience should not only learn information.

They should experience the story.

---

# Storytelling Principles

## 1. Accuracy First

Real events must be separated into:

* Confirmed facts
* Evidence
* Witness accounts
* Theories
* Speculation

No fictional elements should be presented as facts.

---

## 2. Cinematic Presentation

Every story should include:

* Strong opening hook
* Clear narrative structure
* Emotional progression
* Memorable ending

---

## 3. Visual Consistency

Every episode must maintain:

* Character consistency
* Environment consistency
* Historical accuracy
* Cinematic style
